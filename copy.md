## Hero Section

**Title:** The Environmental Impact of Computing: A Research Report

**Subtitle:** Analyzing how computer science both supports climate mitigation and contributes to global energy demands.

**Hero Paragraph 1:**
[cite_start]Our research examines the complex, bidirectional relationship between the computing industry and the environment[cite: 4, 15]. [cite_start]While digital tools like climate modeling and logistics algorithms help reduce carbon emissions, the technology itself accounts for over 2% of global energy demand[cite: 8, 11, 16].

**Hero Paragraph 2:**
[cite_start]This website presents our group’s findings on these intersecting roles[cite: 48]. [cite_start]We aim to provide students and professionals with a clear look at how technology can be a solution to the climate crisis—and where it remains a significant part of the problem[cite: 47, 53].
